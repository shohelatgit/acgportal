Our Story

xScapes was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves an outdoor space that brings joy and enhances their quality of life. What started as a small landscape design service has grown into NoVA's premier full-service landscape architecture firm.

Our team combines artistic vision with technical expertise to create outdoor environments that are both beautiful and functional. From intimate garden courtyards to expansive outdoor living complexes, we approach every project with the same dedication to excellence.

Today, we've transformed hundreds of properties throughout Northern Virginia, earning a reputation for quality craftsmanship, exceptional design, and unwavering customer service.
